Types of Cats

Cats are one of the most beloved household pets. As of 2023, there are almost 400 million cats being kept as pets around the world. Felines are fascinating creatures who can come in all different shapes and sizes. In fact, there are about 73 total cat breeds.

One main characteristic of cats that people use to differentiate between breeds is their fur. Long haired cats are fluffy and require consistent grooming to maintain their coat. On the other hand, short haired cats have a sleek, shiny coat that most cats can maintain themselves. When adopting a cat, it's important to consider each breed and their traits to determine what cat is the best fit for you.
